PARMESAN INFORMATION

Parmesan cheese, specifically Parmigiano-Reggiano, originates from Italy and is produced in specific regions, including Parma, Reggio Emilia, Modena, and parts of Bologna and Mantua.

Authentic Parmigiano-Reggiano is aged for a minimum of 12 months, but many varieties are aged for 24 months or longer. The aging process contributes to its rich flavor and granular texture.

Parmigiano-Reggiano has PDO status, meaning that only cheese produced in certain regions of Italy and following strict regulations can be labeled as Parmigiano-Reggiano.

Parmesan is a good source of protein and calcium, and it is lower in fat compared to many other cheeses. It also contains beneficial nutrients such as vitamins A and B12.
